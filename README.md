# Eniac-Magist

This is a project from the bootcamp I took part in, which I worked on with 3 other students.

ENIAC is a sales company that sells high-tech-products, especially from APPLE.
ENIAC wants to expand their business to the Brazilian market and therefore is looking for a strategic partner, because ENIAC itself has no knowledge of the Brazilian market.

The strategic partner ENIAC is looking at, is called MAGIST.

The task is, to find out if MAGIST is a good fit for ENIAC and their products.

Besides the data, the database and the Tableau-charts, the presentation is showing the conclusion and the answer to the above question.
